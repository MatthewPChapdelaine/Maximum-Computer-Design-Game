## Introduction

Welcome to Maximum PC Builder! This strategic RPG challenges you to build the ultimate high-performance Linux workstation through careful planning, research, and resource management.

## Starting Your Journey

### Initial Resources
- **Money**: $50,000 starting capital
- **Research Points**: 100 points
- **Power Budget**: 2000W (requires PSU upgrades)
- **Passive Income**: $100/tick, 1 research point/tick

### First Steps
1. Review available components in different categories
2. Start with infrastructure (Basic Motherboard, 750W PSU)
3. Research and purchase entry-level components
4. Build up resources before major purchases

## Research System

### How Research Works
1. Select an unresearched component
2. Click "üî¨ Start Research"
3. Wait for research to complete (real-time progress)
4. Component becomes available for purchase

### Research Times
- Basic components: 5-15 seconds
- Mid-tier: 30-60 seconds
- High-end: 90-120 seconds
- Ultimate: 180-360 seconds

### Prerequisites
Most components require owning previous tier components:
- Must own "Budget CPU" before researching "Mid-Range CPU"
- Must own "Mid-Range CPU" before researching "High-End CPU"
- Prerequisites prevent skipping tiers
- Plan your research path carefully

## Building Strategy

### Early Game (0-30 minutes)
1. **Purchase Basic Infrastructure**
   - Basic Motherboard ($150)
   - 750W PSU ($100)
   - Liquid Cooling ($300)

2. **Start Component Research**
   - Research Budget CPU, 16GB DDR4, 500GB SSD
   - Research Budget GPU
   - Research 1Gb Ethernet

3. **Make Initial Purchases**
   - Buy researched components
   - Focus on building passive income

4. **Upgrade PSU Early**
   - 1200W PSU enables more components

### Mid Game (30-90 minutes)
1. **Tier 2-3 Components**
   - Mid-Range CPU ($2,000)
   - 64-256GB RAM
   - Multi-TB NVMe storage
   - Mid-Range GPU

2. **Infrastructure Upgrades**
   - Enthusiast or Workstation Board
   - 2000W PSU
   - Custom Loop Cooling
   - 10Gb Ethernet

3. **Save Regularly**
   - Use üíæ Save Game button
   - Protect your progress

### Late Game (90+ minutes)

   - High-End CPU ($8,000)

3. **Prepare for Ultimate**

## Advanced Tips

### Resource Management
- Don't rush purchases - passive income adds up
- Research multiple components simultaneously is not possible (only one at a time)
- Save before major purchases
- Balance spending across categories

### Power Planning
- Always have PSU headroom
- Calculate power before buying components
- Ultimate build needs 24kW PSU first
- High-end GPUs use 700-1000W each!

### Optimal Progression Path
1. Basic infrastructure first
2. Upgrade PSU before power-hungry components
3. Don't neglect cooling
4. Save up for big purchases rather than incremental buys
5. Research ahead to plan your upgrade path

### Time Management
- Research completes faster for basic components
- Ultimate components take 3-6 minutes to research
- Plan research queue in your head
- Use waiting time to accumulate resources

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Buying components without adequate power
- Skipping infrastructure upgrades
- Ignoring prerequisites
- Not saving regularly
- Spending all money immediately

## FAQ

**Q: Can I research multiple components at once?**
A: No, only one component can be researched at a time.

**Q: What happens if I run out of money?**
A: Passive income continues, so wait for money to accumulate.

**Q: Can I sell components?**
A: Not in version 1.0, but this may be added in future versions.

**Q: How do I save my game?**
A: Click the "üíæ Save Game" button. Saves to `~/.maximum_pc_game_save.json`

**Q: Can I play on Windows or Mac?**
A: Currently Linux only, but the Python code could be adapted.

**Q: Is there a fastest way to win?**
A: Focus on infrastructure early, save aggressively, and make smart upgrade decisions.

**Q: What's the minimum time to complete?**
A: With perfect play, approximately 2-3 hours due to research times and resource accumulation.
